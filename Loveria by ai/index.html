How Our Story Began

25 Oct 2022
We first met during Kali Puja — a festival of lights that unknowingly lit something else too.
13 May 2023
We were both deeply impressed by each other. That day, we spent hours looking at one another, creating a special memory that stayed with us.
Rath Mela 2023
We met again at the Rath Mela, though 2023 ended before we could meet once more.
14 Feb 2024
Saraswati Puja. I wanted so badly to talk to her, but she was with her two younger sisters. The words stayed inside.
Charak Puja 2024
Another meeting. The universe kept finding ways to bring us back together.
The Facebook Chapter
After that, I created a fake Facebook account in her name and sent a message to her father's account. Her elder sister replied and blocked me. I tried again with another account, and this time her sister agreed not to block my real account
A Hopeful Message
One day, I unexpectedly received a message from her father's account. It was Parboti. That was our first conversation, and she asked me to meet her the following Monday.
20 May 2024
Our first date. I brought chocolates. First time we talked face to face for nearly two hours — families, dreams, sadness, laughter. Everything except the three words we both wanted to say.
15 July 2024
I had decided to propose to her on what we believed was her birthday. Although we had met many times by then, neither of us had confessed our feelings, even though we both wanted to give our relationship a name.
I bought a large bouquet of flowers and planned to propose. However, the friend who was supposed to accompany me never arrived, and because I felt shy in front of her friends, I couldn't do it.
We couldn't meet for the next week, and sadly, I eventually had to throw away the bouquet.

(Note. We also discovered her real birthday is 29 June — a tiny error on her Aadhaar card that we now know better.)
29 July 2024 · 7:10 PM
After our tuition classes, I proposed to her with a small rose, That was enough. Around 7:10–7:20 in the evening, and from that moment, we finally had a name for what we were.
And beyond…
Arguments, deep talks, a cheek kiss, a first lip kiss months later. And still — still — together. Still growing.
